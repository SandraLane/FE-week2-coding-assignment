Stuttgart Things to Do

Porsche Museum. Houses over 80 vehicles. Open everyday except Mondays and Holidays. Location: northern Stuttgart subarb of Zuffenhausen.
Mercedes Benz Museum. More than 160 vehicles. Open everyday except Mondays and Holidays. Location: 4 miles from downtown Stuttgart.
Wilhelma Zoo. 70 acres of parkland. Houses around 10,000 animals. Open year-round.
Konigstrasse. Central commercial avenue of Stuttgart. Known for its sparkling jewelry shops and dining options. Admire beautiful works of art in its Kunstmuseum Stuttgart.
Schlossplatz. Browse markets. Attend live musical performances. Public baroque-style park.
